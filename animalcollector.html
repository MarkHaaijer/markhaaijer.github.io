Animal Collector

Animal Collector is a work in progress title for a 2D open world exploration adventure game.

This is a project I'm currently working on that puts an emphasis on being creative, as you explore the landscapes you'll be finding items and taming/befriending animals that allow you to create different tools that help you move around and discover new area's while providing minigames sprinkled in.
